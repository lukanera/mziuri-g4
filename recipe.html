Delicous lobiani

ingredients

ingredients quantity
beans 1kg
flour 1kg
dry yeast 1tsb
onions 2
salt to taste
peper to taste
butter to grease
sunflower oil 150ml

instructions

  1. Filling: Wash and boil beans. When beans are ready strain off extra water and smash or process beans through meat grinder. Meanwhile fry chopped or grated onion in 100 ml of oil, then add it to the mashed beans, season well and mix up.
  2. Dough: Mix flour, 50 gr of oil, salt and pinch of sugar. Add warm water little by little continuously mixing ingredients. When dough is elastic and smooth cover and leave it for 1-2 hours to rise.
  3. Divide dough into 7-8 parts, shape balls and let them rest for a while. Roll out each ball, place filling in the center, fold and flatten to 1 centimeter thickness using rolling pin and your hands. Bake round-shaped lobiani in the oven at 180 degrees until slightly golden-brown or in the pan from both sides. When it is ready brush lobiani with butter and enjoy.

notes

Don't overcook it or undercook it and to do so you must watch it while you cook it, a timer might help to not forget.
